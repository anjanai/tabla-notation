  • Kamali chakkardar has a total of 27 dhaa (3 dhaa in each repetition of the tihai that makes it 9, then the whole composition is played 3 times as chakkardar for 27). The 1st, 14th and 27th dhaa land on the sum (see dhaa highlighted in red above)
  • 1st dhaa is the frst dhaa of the frst repetition of the tihai of the frst chakra
  • 14th dhaa is the second dhaa of the second repetition of the tihai of the second chakra
  • 27th dhaa is the third dhaa of the third repetition of the tihai of the third chakra
