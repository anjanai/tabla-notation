Tihai with a metronome

Practice playing this on your tabla with various speeds before you move on to the next table. Synchronize your beats with the tabla. For the pauses, just tap your index finger lightly on the bayan.

dhaa - dhaa ge naa dhaa
- dhaa ti dhaa ge dhin naa ge naa dhaa
- dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa
- dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa
- dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa -

The same thing now two beats at a time. Again practice this on your tabla with various speeds before moving on.

dhaa - dhaa ge naa dhaa
- dhaa ti dhaa ge dhin naa ge naa dhaa
- dhaa - dhaa - ta
dhaa - dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa
- dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa -

The same thing now four beats at a time. This is just one iteration. Practice this till you are completely comfortable, before moving on to doing the three iterations all together.

dhaa - dhaa ge naa dhaa - dhaa ti dhaa ge dhin naa ge naa dhaa - dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa - dhaa - dhaa -
ta dhaa - dhaa - dhaa - ta dhaa -
